Owl

Owls have asymmetrical Ears meaning one ear is slightly higher than the other, This is purposely to give them a more acoustic hearing of their surroundings which can help them find prey easier.

  • Scientific Name: Strigiformes
  • Average Lifespan: 5-10 years
  • Average Wingspan: 4.6 feet
  • Average Speed: 20-30 mph

Generally Most species of owls will have a Large broad head with large eyes & a stocky body. Most typical Owls have a round face while owls like the Barn owl will have a more heart-shaped face.
